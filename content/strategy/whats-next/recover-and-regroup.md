Priority one: get healthy and mobile again. We nearly lost two people today.

## Tactics

1. **Find shelter**
   - Return to the keep (the guards are dead, but the structure is secure)
   - Or secure a building in town
2. **Short or long rest**
   - Short rest: 1 hour, spend hit dice to heal
   - Long rest: 8 hours, full recovery but risky
3. **Recover the horses**
   - Search the battlefield for a whistle or horn
   - If not found, Theren tracks them
   - Nysera might be able to communicate with animals
4. **Divide the loot**
   - Ring of Jumping goes to someone who needs mobility
   - Split the gold and gems

## Pros
- Restores spell slots and HP
- Kaltor and Theren were nearly dead - we need recovery
- Horses mean mobility if we need to leave fast

## Cons
- Time passes - whatever's happening continues
- Enemies might regroup or reinforcements arrive
- Questions remain unanswered

## Best If
The party is too wounded to do much else safely.

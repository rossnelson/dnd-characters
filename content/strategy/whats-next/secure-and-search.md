Stay in Nightstone. Secure the area, search the bodies, and investigate what's really going on here.

## Tactics

1. **Secure the perimeter**
   - Theren scouts for any remaining threats
   - We're alone now - no guards to post, just us
2. **Search the battlefield**
   - Loot the bodies systematically
   - Look for any documents, maps, or orders on the bandits/orcs
   - Check for a whistle or horn to recall the horses
3. **Interrogate survivors** (if any)
   - Elaris leads questioning (CHA +4)
   - Focus on Kella's network and the orc timing
4. **Explore Nightstone further**
   - What else is in this town?
   - Any buildings we haven't checked?
   - Where did the original inhabitants go?

## Pros
- Maximizes intel gathering
- Ensures no threats remain
- Might find more loot or clues
- Gives time to rest and heal

## Cons
- **The snake got away** - someone knows the bandits failed. Clock is ticking.
- Takes time - if reinforcements are coming, we're sitting ducks
- Nightstone may not have what we need
- The horses are still out there

## Best If
You want answers about what's happening here before moving on.

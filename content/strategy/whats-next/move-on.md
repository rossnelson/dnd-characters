Nightstone is a ruin. The people who lived here are gone. Maybe it's time we were too.

## Tactics

1. **Quick search**
   - Grab any obvious loot
   - Look for the horse whistle
   - Don't linger
2. **Search the keep**
   - The guards are dead, but they might have left notes or maps
   - Check for supplies before leaving
3. **Pick a direction**
   - Where did the Nightstone villagers flee to?
   - Is there a larger town nearby?
   - Follow up on any leads about Kella's contacts
4. **Travel**
   - With horses if we can get them back
   - On foot if we must

## Pros
- **The snake escaped** - whoever Kella reported to knows. Best not to be here when they respond.
- Gets us out of a ruined town with no resources
- Moves toward civilization and answers
- Less chance of another ambush here

## Cons
- Leaves questions unanswered
- Might miss important clues
- Without horses, travel is slow and dangerous

## Best If
Nightstone has nothing more to offer and we have a lead to follow.

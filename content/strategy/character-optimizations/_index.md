# Character Optimization Suggestions

These guides are based on the party's current build choices, how those choices played out in recent combat, and what the cave environment ahead is likely to demand.

Nothing here is mandatory. These are suggestions informed by party composition - the goal is to highlight synergies and flag choices that aren't pulling their weight so you can make informed decisions at the next level-up or spell-swap opportunity.

Each guide covers priority changes first, then usage tips for what you already have, and a brief note on the next level-up.

# Theren Vale - Optimization Guide

## Current Build Summary

**Wood Elf Hunter Ranger 3** | DEX 18 (+4) | WIS 17 (+3) | CON 15 (+2)

HP: 28 | Spells: Cure Wounds, Hunter's Mark, Detect Magic, Speak with Animals

Theren has excellent ability scores. The problem this session wasn't stats - it was gear and spell choices. Scale mail cost him the scout, and the survival spells are underused.

## Priority Changes

### 1. Upgrade Armor: Scale Mail to Breastplate

Scale mail: AC 14 + DEX max 2 = 16 AC, **disadvantage on stealth**.

Breastplate: AC 13 + DEX max 2 = 15 AC... but with DEX 18 you get the full +4, making it AC 17. **No stealth disadvantage.**

Theren rolled a natural 20 on stealth and still failed because scale mail forced a second roll. A breastplate ends that problem entirely and actually gives +1 AC over scale mail when accounting for full DEX bonus. Breastplate costs 400 GP - Theren currently has 302 GP, 23 SP. Close, and worth prioritizing.

### 2. Prepare Absorb Elements Instead of Cure Wounds

Absorb Elements (1st-level, reaction): when you take acid, cold, fire, lightning, or thunder damage, reduce that damage by half and add 1d6 of that damage type to your next attack.

Theren dropped to 0 from a single crit + normal hit in round 1. Cure Wounds requires a full action and is inferior to Healing Word at the same slot level (Healing Word is a bonus action). Absorb Elements is a reaction that could have cut that ogre damage in half when it mattered. Against cave environments with fire, ice, or lightning-using enemies, it has even more value.

### 3. Hunter's Mark Management

Hunter's Mark is Theren's core damage spell - 1d6 bonus damage on every attack against the marked target. But it requires concentration, which conflicts with nothing else Theren has. The issue is:

- Mark the highest-HP enemy, not the first one you see
- When the marked target dies, use a **bonus action** to transfer the mark (no slot cost)
- If you lose concentration, re-casting costs another slot

Do not drop Hunter's Mark voluntarily. Keep it going for the whole fight.

## Spell Usage Guide

| Spell | Usage |
|-------|-------|
| **Hunter's Mark** | Cast before or in round 1. Transfer on kill. Primary damage multiplier. |
| **Absorb Elements** | Reaction to incoming elemental damage. Half damage + bonus melee hit. |
| **Detect Magic** | Ritual cast (10 min, no slot) for exploration. Do not use a slot for this. |
| **Speak with Animals** | Ritual-capable if you have the feature; otherwise situational. |

## Scouting Protocol

Until the armor situation is resolved, consider the following when scouting:

- Remove scale mail before scouting if time permits (5-10 minute reequip)
- Alternatively, request Nysera prepare Pass Without Trace (concentration, +10 stealth to all nearby, no disadvantage override) before the next deep cave push
- Scout with Favored Foe or advantage from other sources when possible

## Next Level-Up Plan

Level 4: **+2 DEX takes you from 18 to 20 (+5)**. That's +1 to attack rolls, +1 to damage with Archery, +1 to AC with the breastplate (18 AC total), and +1 to DEX saves/checks. Rangers are DEX-dependent - this is the strongest single investment available. No feat competes with it at this level unless you have a specific build goal.

At level 5, Extra Attack doubles your damage output. Hunter's Mark on an Extra Attack build is very strong - two attacks, both applying the 1d6 mark bonus.

# Selena - Optimization Guide

## Current Build Summary

**Half-Elf Life Domain Cleric 3** | WIS 17 (+3) | CHA 16 (+3) | INT 13 (+1)

Spells: Bless, Healing Word, Sanctuary, Guiding Bolt, Sacred Flame, Guidance, Thaumaturgy, Mending

Life Domain gives Disciple of Life (healing spells restore 2 + spell level bonus HP), Channel Divinity: Preserve Life, and bonus spell access. Selena is the party's primary healer, but the current spell choices lean too heavily on Sacred Flame when better damage options are available.

## Priority Changes

### 1. Replace Sacred Flame with Toll the Dead

Sacred Flame: DEX save, no damage on success. The ogre passed its DEX save and took nothing.

Toll the Dead: WIS save, 1d8 necrotic (or 1d12 if the target is already missing HP). Ogres have poor WIS. Necrotic damage is rarely resisted. A wounded ogre failing a WIS save takes 1d12 - better damage, better targeting, better against the actual enemies in this dungeon.

Sacred Flame is not a bad cantrip. Toll the Dead is better in this environment specifically.

### 2. Prepare Spiritual Weapon (Bonus Action Damage)

Spiritual Weapon (2nd-level, non-concentration): summon a floating weapon as a bonus action. Each subsequent turn, move it and attack as a bonus action. WIS-based attack roll, 1d8+WIS damage.

This is Selena's most efficient damage output. It doesn't require concentration (Bless or Sanctuary can stay up), it persists for 1 minute, and it frees actions for healing words or support. Should be prepared every session.

### 3. Understand the Life Domain + Goodberry Combo

If anyone in the party casts Goodberry, Selena's Disciple of Life feature applies: each berry restores 1+2+spell level (1st) = 4 HP instead of 1 HP. Ten berries from one spell slot becomes 40 HP of healing.

Nysera is a druid - Goodberry is on the druid spell list. If Nysera prepares Goodberry before the next long rest, each casting of Goodberry provides 40 HP of out-of-combat healing for the cost of one 1st-level slot. This is the most efficient healing in the game at these levels.

## Spell Usage Guide

| Spell | Usage |
|-------|-------|
| **Healing Word** | Bonus action, 60ft range. Primary revival tool. Use when an ally goes down mid-combat. Never cast this as your main action - save the action for damage or control. |
| **Bless** | Opening move if Elaris hasn't cast it. Don't double-cast. Check first. |
| **Guiding Bolt** | Round 1 before melee closes. 4d6 radiant + advantage to next attacker. Strongest damage spell in the list - use it early. |
| **Sanctuary** | On yourself or an ally before entering a chokepoint. Enemies must save to target you. Combined with 20 HP and Life Domain, Selena can stay in the back safely. |
| **Toll the Dead** | Primary damage cantrip. Target low-WIS enemies. Use on enemies already wounded for the d12 upgrade. |

## Channel Divinity: Preserve Life

Restore HP to any number of creatures within 30 feet, up to a combined total of 5 × cleric level (15 HP at level 3). Each creature can be healed up to half their max HP. No action cost beyond the Channel Divinity use.

Use this during a short rest or immediately after a hard fight, not in combat when Healing Word is more efficient per action.

## Next Level-Up Plan

Level 4: +2 WIS takes you to 19 (+4). Better healing, better spell DCs, better saves. With WIS 18+ the spell DC goes to 15, which makes Toll the Dead and any control spells significantly harder to resist. This is the clearest ASI path for Selena's role.

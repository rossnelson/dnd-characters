## Darkvision Roster

Every member of this party has darkvision. No light source is required for vision.

| Character | Race | Darkvision Range |

All darkvision is 60ft. You can see 60ft in magical and non-magical darkness. Beyond 60ft: nothing. This means the cave beyond your vision radius is a complete unknown - threats can exist just outside your sight.

### Darkvision Limitation Note
60ft darkvision means an enemy at 61ft is invisible to you. In an open cavern, a ranged threat - a goblin archer, a caster, something with a bow - can fire at you from outside your darkvision range without being visible. Stay aware of that blind zone.

Before advancing a single step, Selena must use Healing Word on Barzer. At 4 HP he is a liability - not because he's weak, but because a stray arrow or goblin attack drops him instantly. Selena's Life Domain healing bonus makes even a 1st-level Healing Word substantial.

Do this before the party moves.

## Tactical Reality Change: Open Cavern vs. Bottleneck

The bottleneck is behind you. You have now lost your primary defensive advantage.

In the bottleneck:
- Enemies could only reach you 1-2 at a time
- Kaltor could lock down the entire enemy advance solo

In an open cavern:
- Enemies can spread out and attack from multiple angles
- The party can be surrounded
- The missing ogre could be flanking you right now

This changes everything about how you engage. See the updated strategy documents for open-cavern tactics.

## The Missing Ogre Problem

Treat it as hostile until proven otherwise. Assume it knows you're here.

One ogre down, the other bloodied and running. The smart play is to hold position and regroup. But Theren and Elaris look at each other and make the call: chase. They push into the dark after a wounded ogre, leaving the rest of the party behind. The session ends mid-sprint, deep in a cave, with unknown threats ahead and their backup still catching up. Sometimes the best moments are the worst decisions.

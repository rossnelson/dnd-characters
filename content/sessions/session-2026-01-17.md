# The Battle of Nightstone

## The Confrontation

We crossed the moat to confront Kella's bandits on the town side of Nightstone. Whatever diplomacy might have been possible evaporated quickly - steel was drawn and the fight began.

Then the horns sounded.

## The Orc Raid

Mid-battle, an orc war band descended on Nightstone. The war chief blew his horn, rallying his troops as they crashed into the fight. Suddenly the bandits weren't our only problem - they weren't even our biggest problem.

In a twist of battlefield logic, everyone turned to face the greater threat. Orcs don't negotiate.

## The Fight

**Barzer** took to the air, raining fire from 45 feet up. Fire Bolt and Magic Missile tore into the orc war chief while he tried to coordinate from the back lines.

**Kaltor** charged into the thick of it, trading blows with multiple orcs. He held the line until he couldn't anymore.

**Theren** took position on a boulder for the high ground advantage, picking targets with his longbow and Hunter's Mark. An orc spear found him - 21 points of damage when he only had 16 left. He went down hard.

**Elaris** kept her head when others were losing theirs. Her Healing Word brought Theren back to consciousness just as he was fading.

**Kaltor went down too** - overwhelmed by orc fury. But the party rallied.

**Selena and Elaris** worked overtime on healing, pulling our frontliners back from the brink.

**Nysera** controlled the battlefield, making every spell count.

## The Aftermath

One by one, the orcs fell. The moment the last orc dropped, **Selena** wasted no time - she turned and immediately engaged the bandits, landing a strong hit on the bandit leader before they could even react.

What followed was chaos. **Elaris found herself surrounded** by bandits closing in. But before they could capitalize, **Theren** made his move - he leapt off the boulder, sprinted toward the bandit leader, marked him with Hunter's Mark, and unleashed a flurry of short sword strikes with two-weapon fighting. Both blades found their mark, leaving the bandit leader bloodied and reeling.

Then **Elaris** stepped up with a nat 20, delivering a devastating critical hit that ended any hope the bandits had of regrouping. After that, it was over quickly.

We're bruised. We're bloodied. But we're standing.

**Kaltor and Theren both tasted death today** - and spat it back out thanks to the healers. That's twice in one fight our front line nearly collapsed.

### The Snake Got Away

As the dust settled, movement caught our eyes - **Kella's flying snake** was fleeing the battlefield. Barzer hurled a Fire Bolt after it, but the spell went wide. The snake disappeared into the distance.

Someone is going to know what happened here. The question is: who, and what will they do about it?

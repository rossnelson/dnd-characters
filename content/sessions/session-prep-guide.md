# Player Session Prep Checklist

## 15 Minutes Before Session

### Character Ready (5 min)
- [ ] Review your character sheet - abilities, spells, features
- [ ] Check HP, spell slots, and resources from last session
- [ ] Update inventory - what did you pick up/use last time?
- [ ] Complete any pending level-ups before session starts
- [ ] Have dice, notepad, and spell cards ready

### Story Refresh (3 min)
- [ ] Review your notes from last session
- [ ] Remember where the party left off
- [ ] Check any personal quests or goals you're pursuing
- [ ] Note any NPCs you want to follow up with
- [ ] Review any unfinished party business

### Character Mindset (2 min)
- [ ] Remember your character's motivations and personality
- [ ] Think about how recent events affected your character
- [ ] Consider what your character wants to accomplish this session
- [ ] Review bonds with other party members

### Practical Setup (5 min)
- [ ] Snacks and drinks ready (quiet ones during play)
- [ ] Phone on silent
- [ ] Bookmark any rules you often need to reference
- [ ] Have spell descriptions ready if you're a caster
- [ ] Character sheet easily accessible (physical or digital)

## During Session

### Combat Efficiency
- Plan your turn while others are taking theirs
- Have a default action ready if you're unsure
- Know your bonus actions and reactions
- Track your own HP and resources
- Call out your total damage (include type)

### Roleplay Engagement
- Take notes on important NPCs and locations
- Ask questions in character
- Build on other players' ideas
- Share the spotlight
- Stay in character when possible

### Party Cooperation
- Track shared resources (who has healing potions?)
- Communicate your character's abilities to help planning
- Support other players' character moments
- Discuss plans in character when possible

## Between Sessions

### Character Development
- [ ] Update backstory based on session events
- [ ] Write session notes while memory is fresh
- [ ] Level up if you gained experience
- [ ] Shopping/inventory management if in town
- [ ] Communicate with party about plans

### Table Communication
- [ ] Let DM know ASAP if you'll be late/absent
- [ ] Share any character goals with the DM
- [ ] Participate in group chat planning
- [ ] Provide feedback if something isn't working

## Quick Reference

### Theren Vale Personal Notes
- **Core Values**: Actions over words, protect the innocent
- **Personality**: Suspicious of authority, loyal to proven allies
- **Goals**: Find redemption, help common folk
- **Fears**: Being manipulated again, causing harm through inaction
- **Bonds**: Growing trust with party members
- **Flaws**: Too quick to judge, holds grudges

### Action Economy Reminder
- **Action**: Attack, Cast a spell, Dash, Dodge, Help, Hide, Ready, Search
- **Bonus Action**: Only if ability/spell grants one
- **Reaction**: Opportunity attacks, Ready trigger, specific abilities
- **Movement**: Can split before/after/during actions
- **Free**: Draw/sheathe weapon, open door, speak

### Skill Check Guidelines
- Ask "Can I try..." before rolling
- Describe how you're attempting it
- Multiple attempts usually not allowed
- Help action gives advantage
- Passive scores always active

### Rest Reminders
- **Short Rest** (1 hour): Roll HD to heal, regain some abilities
- **Long Rest** (8 hours): Full HP, all HD back, spell slots reset
- Can't benefit from long rest more than once per 24 hours

## Session Etiquette
- Arrive on time or notify the group
- Pay attention during others' turns
- Avoid phone unless for game purposes
- Don't talk over the DM or other players
- Celebrate others' successes
- Accept failure as part of the story
- Thank your DM after session

## Post-Session (5 min after)
- [ ] Write quick notes on what happened
- [ ] Note any items or gold received
- [ ] Update character sheet with XP/milestone
- [ ] Plan any between-session character activities
- [ ] Schedule availability for next session
